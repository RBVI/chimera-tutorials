Exploratory Visualization

  • Exploratory visualizations
    • Visualization tools that allow the user to explore the data without constraints to a particular view or hypothesis.
    • Often very interactive:
      • Multiple views/representations
      • Heavy use of interaction techniques:
        • Searching, filtering, brushing, linking
    • Often want to integrate additional data from other experiments or public resources
    • Often linked with analyses
