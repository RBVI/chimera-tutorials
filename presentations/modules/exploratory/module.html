Exploratory Visualization

  • Explanatory visualizations
    • Visual artifacts that are used in support of (explain) a hypothesis or result
    • Emphasis is on clarity of visualization and use of visual intuition
    • Might have interactive components, but generally limited to avoid leading the observer away from the main point
  • In the "Goodwin" lexicon:
    • Not quite "decision support", but not really simply "to impress"
    • Purpose is to convince you (without visualization malpractice) of a conclusion

Exploratory Visualization

  • Exploratory visualizations
    • Visualization tools that allow the user to explore the data without constraints to a particular view or hypothesis.
    • Often very interactive:
      • Multiple views/representations
      • Heavy use of interaction techniques:
        • Searching, filtering, brushing, linking, overview+detail
    • Often want to integrate additional data from other experiments or public resources
    • Often linked with analyses
