Pre-Course Instructions

STEP 1: PPI Image

  • Prepare a single image that exemplifies the importance of protein-protein interactions in your mind.
    • You'll have the opportunity to explain your image during the sesion.
    • It can be a figure from a paper, an image from your own work, or something that you created.
  • Send the image to me via email with the subject: PPI Image. Please include your name in the email.
  • Bring the image to the course and be prepared to explain the image to others.
