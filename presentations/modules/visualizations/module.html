Visual Representations

  • Heatmaps: expression data
    • Clustered, Self organizing, tabular
  • Sequence tracks: mutational analysis, epigenetics
  • Circos plots: comparative genomics, cellular localization
  • Networks: interactions and similarities
    • Adjacency matrices
    • Hive plots
    • Node-link diagrams (hairball?)
  • ...and many others...
    • Manhattan plots: GenomeWide Association Studies (GWAS)
    • Volcano plots, Violin plots: Differential expression
    • Scatter plots: PCA, t-SNE
    • Charts (line, bar, pie, etc.)
      • (small multiples)
