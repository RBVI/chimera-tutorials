Experimental techniques

  • Xray crystallography
  • “Gold standard” for structure determination
  • Pros:
    • High resolution
  • Cons:
    • Not all proteins can be crystallized
    • Protein contacts can distort proteins
    • Proteins are not in solution
    • Only see one conformation

Experimental techniques

  • Xray crystallography
  • NMR
  • CryoEM
  • Pros:
    • High resolution
    • No need for crystal
    • No need for high concentrated solution
    • No need for protein modification
  • Cons:
    • Complex measurement and data analysis
    • Hard for small proteins
